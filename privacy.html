Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

Snipkit ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our macOS application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Snipkit is designed with privacy in mind. All your data, including:

  • Text snippets you create
  • Categories you organize them into
  • App preferences and settings

...is stored locally on your Mac and never leaves your device.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your Mac using Apple's secure data storage mechanisms. We do not have access to your snippets or any other data you create within the app.

No Analytics or Tracking

Snipkit does not include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry. We do not track:

  • How you use the app
  • What snippets you create
  • When or how often you use the app
  • Any device identifiers

No Third-Party Services

Snipkit does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or data brokers. Your data stays on your device.

No Internet Connection Required

Snipkit works entirely offline. The app does not require an internet connection to function, and it does not transmit any data over the network.

App Store

Snipkit is distributed through the Mac App Store. Apple may collect certain information as described in Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to this information.

Children's Privacy

Snipkit does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not collect any personal information from any users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
